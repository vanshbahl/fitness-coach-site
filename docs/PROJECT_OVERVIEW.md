# Project Overview: Quick Strength

## Project Vision
Quick Strength is a premium trial booking platform built for Abhay Pandey. The application discards the traditional "landing page + form" model in favor of an interactive, app-like onboarding experience. The booking process *is* the product. Users are guided through a sleek, multi-step assessment that feels like joining an exclusive program, culminating in seamless payment and automated calendar integration. The website's responsibility ends completely once the trial session has been successfully booked. Everything after the trial session is handled manually and personally by Abhay.

## Business Goals
- **Maximize Conversion**: Replace intimidating long-forms with engaging, bite-sized onboarding steps.
- **Premium Brand Perception**: Every interaction, slider, and micro-animation should feel like an elite fitness app (think Nike, Linear, Gymshark).
- **Zero Friction**: Achieve sub-90 second booking times optimized entirely for one-handed mobile use (Instagram traffic).
- **Total Automation**: Automatically handle scheduling, payments, and calendar invites.

## Official User Journey (Version 1 Scope)
1. **Instagram / Social Media**
2. **Landing Page**
3. **Interactive Assessment**: Multi-step wizard capturing personal details, body metrics, and goals.
4. **₹49 Trial Payment (Razorpay)**: Payment occurs *before* slot confirmation to prevent unpaid users from blocking appointment slots.
5. **Choose Available Time Slot**
6. **Booking Confirmation**
7. **Google Calendar Event Created**: Invites Abhay and the User. Google handles all reminders.
8. **Google Meet Link Automatically Generated**
9. **Confirmation Emails Sent**: Delivered via Resend.
10. **Trial Session**
11. **END OF WEBSITE RESPONSIBILITY**: After the trial, Abhay manually communicates with the user and manually adds them to the coaching group if they continue.

## MVP Scope (Admin)
The MVP focuses purely on the onboarding funnel, conversion, and minimal administrative management.
- **Admin Dashboard**: Includes only Bookings, Payment Status, Trial Status, Calendar Status, Assessment Data, Coach Notes, Search, and Filters.
- **Excluded in V1**: No subscriptions, no memberships, no student portal, no client dashboard, no workout tracking, no attendance, no CRM features, no community features.
