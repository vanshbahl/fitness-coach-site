# Project Overview: Quick Strength

## Project Vision
Quick Strength aims to provide a premium, seamless digital experience for discovering fitness coaching, booking a trial session, and establishing an initial relationship with the coach. Moving away from manual Google Form workflows, this platform will automate scheduling, payments, and communications, elevating the brand's professionalism and user trust.

## Business Goals
- **Automate Workflows**: Eliminate manual scheduling and payment verification.
- **Increase Conversions**: Provide a frictionless, high-trust booking experience that encourages users to commit to a trial session.
- **Brand Elevation**: Present a modern, premium web presence that aligns with the quality of the coaching provided.
- **Operational Efficiency**: Centralize booking data and status tracking for the coach.

## User Types
1. **Student / Client (Public User)**: Individuals looking to learn calisthenics, understand its benefits, and book a trial session.
2. **Coach / Admin (Abhay Pandey)**: The owner of the platform who manages bookings, tracks payments, marks attendance, and monitors business metrics.

## MVP Scope
The Minimum Viable Product focuses exclusively on the trial session booking funnel and fundamental administrative capabilities.
- **Public Website**: Landing page with Hero, About Coach, Why Calisthenics, Benefits, Testimonials, FAQ, and Book Trial CTA.
- **Booking Flow**: Slot selection, personal detail entry, ₹49 Razorpay integration, success confirmation, and automated Google Calendar event creation.
- **Admin Dashboard**: View all bookings, track payment statuses, mark attendance, add notes, and convert trial clients to enrolled students.
- **Transactional Emails**: Automated confirmations and receipts via Resend.

## Future Scope
While not in the MVP, the architecture should not prohibit the following future features:
- Recurring subscription payments for enrolled students.
- Client portal for tracking progress, workout plans, and nutrition.
- Rescheduling and cancellation workflows.
- Multi-coach support if the business expands.
- Automated WhatsApp notifications.

## Success Criteria
- 100% automated booking and payment verification process.
- 0 manual interventions required for scheduling a trial session on Google Calendar.
- Page load times under 2 seconds for the public landing page.
- Fully responsive design (mobile-first approach).
